Navitech ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Buckt mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

By using Buckt, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

- Email address (for account creation, login, syncing data across devices, support communications, and optional newsletters)
- Budgeting and financial data entered into the App
- Images you submit using camera features for app functionality
- Information related to premium subscription purchases (excluding payment card details)

### b. Payment Information

Premium purchases may be processed through third-party payment processors such as Stripe or applicable app store billing providers, including Google Play Billing.

We do not collect or store your payment card numbers or full payment credentials.

### c. Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect limited technical and usage information, such as:

- Device type and operating system
- App usage analytics
- Crash reports and diagnostics
- Device identifiers used for analytics and notifications
- Push notification tokens

This information may be collected through services such as Firebase, Mixpanel, Sentry, and Expo.

## 2. Camera and Uploaded Images

If you use camera-based features, images you capture may be uploaded to our servers solely to provide the related app functionality.

We do not use uploaded images for advertising, profiling, or unrelated purposes.

## 3. How We Use Information

We may use information collected to:

- Provide and operate app features
- Authenticate accounts and synchronize user data
- Improve usability, performance, and product development
- Analyze usage patterns and fix technical issues
- Send reminders and push notifications related to app use
- Provide customer support
- Send newsletters or product updates (where you opt in, with unsubscribe options)
- Process premium features and subscriptions
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Advertising

Buckt may display advertisements through providers such as Google Ads.

We use non-personalized ads, and we do not engage in cross-app or cross-site behavioral tracking.
