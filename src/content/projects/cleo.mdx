## The Problem

Keeping a home clean is easy to forget. People want to stay on top of it but struggle to fit cleaning into their schedule — and lose track of when they last cleaned each spot. Most cleaning apps either feel overwhelming or punish you for missing a task.

## The Solution

Cleo tracks cleaning tasks per spot in your home, suggests a routine schedule based on your habits, and never punishes missed tasks. It's designed to make catching up feel easy and guilt-free, not like a chore pile-up.

## Stack

- **Frontend**: Flutter (Android + iOS)
- **Local Storage**: SQLite
- **Sync**: Firestore (real-time cloud sync)

## Features

**Built:**
- Task tracking per spot in the home
- Local-first storage with SQLite — works offline
- Real-time Firestore sync across devices

**Coming soon:**
- Routine schedule suggestions based on cleaning habits
- Schedule intelligence (adaptive reminders, streak tracking)
- Overdue task catch-up mode

## Key Challenges

- Designing a local-first sync architecture that stays consistent between SQLite and Firestore without conflicts
- Keeping the UX guilt-free by design — rethinking how most task apps handle missed or overdue items
